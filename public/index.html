How Promoters & Creators Connect

From discovery to campaign completion — streamlined collaboration in four simple steps

📢 For Promoters

Launch campaigns effortlessly and let AI handle the heavy lifting of creator discovery and management.

  • Smart Discovery: AI analyzes creator profiles and promoter requirements to find perfect matches based on audience demographics, engagement rates, and niche alignment.
  • Contract & Terms: Automated contract generation with customizable terms, deliverables, timelines, and payment structures. Legal templates ensure both parties are protected.
  • Campaign Analytics: Real-time performance tracking with detailed insights on reach, engagement, conversions, and ROI. Automated reporting for both parties.

🎨 For Creators

Get matched with perfect brands and receive AI-powered content guidance to maximize your creative potential.

  • Intelligent Matching: Get discovered by brands that align with your content style, audience, and values. No more endless pitching or mismatched collaborations.
  • Content Creation: Receive AI-generated briefs with trend insights, content ideas, and performance optimization suggestions tailored to your style and audience.
  • Seamless Workflow: Auto-generated briefs, streamlined approvals, and tracked deliverables — faster execution with built-in revision and approval systems.
